About SESB-MUN 2026

Welcome to SESB-MUN 2026

SESB-MUN (SESB Model United Nations) is a prestigious Model UN conference hosted by Dreilinden-Gymnasium Berlin, bringing together over 100 delegates from schools across Germany and beyond. Our conference offers an immersive diplomatic experience in the vibrant city of Berlin.

From July 1–3, 2026, delegates will engage in intense debates, draft resolutions, and work collaboratively to address pressing global issues. Our carefully designed committees cater to both beginners and experienced MUN participants.

UN Security Council

Committee Overview

The United Nations Security Council (UNSC) is one of the six principal organs of the United Nations, charged with ensuring international peace and security. At SESB-MUN 2026, the Security Council will address pressing global security challenges with a special focus on emerging cyber warfare threats.

Agenda Items:

  • Addressing the threat of cyber warfare to international peace and security
  • The situation in the South China Sea and maritime security
  • Crisis simulation (details to be announced during conference)

Committee Details:

  • 15 Member States (5 permanent, 10 non-permanent)
  • Rules of Procedure: Modified UN4MUN
  • Chairs: TBA

GA1 - DISEC

Committee Overview

The Disarmament and International Security Committee (DISEC) is the First Committee of the United Nations General Assembly. It deals with disarmament, global challenges and threats to peace that affect the international community and seeks out solutions to the challenges in the international security regime.

Agenda Items:

  • Effective disarmament in post-conflict zones
  • Nuclear non-proliferation and the future of arms control agreements
  • The militarization of artificial intelligence and autonomous weapons systems

Committee Details:

  • 30–40 Member States
  • Rules of Procedure: Standard UN4MUN
  • Chairs: TBA

GA3 - SOCHUM

Committee Overview

The Social, Humanitarian and Cultural Committee (SOCHUM) is the Third Committee of the United Nations General Assembly. SOCHUM focuses on human rights, humanitarian affairs and social matters of international character. At SESB-MUN 2026, SOCHUM will address pressing humanitarian challenges including combating human trafficking.

Agenda Items:

  • Combating human trafficking in conflict and post-conflict situations
  • Addressing the global refugee crisis and protecting displaced populations
  • Preserving cultural heritage in conflict zones

Committee Details:

  • 30–40 Member States
  • Rules of Procedure: Standard UN4MUN
  • Chairs: TBA

GA6 - LEGAL

Committee Overview

The Sixth Committee of the United Nations General Assembly is the primary forum for the consideration of legal questions in the General Assembly. At SESB-MUN 2026, the Legal Committee will examine the continued feasibility of the right to asylum in the 21st century.

Agenda Items:

  • The continued feasibility of the right to asylum in the 21st century
  • Legal frameworks for addressing climate displacement
  • The responsibility to protect (R2P) and its implementation challenges

Committee Details:

  • 30–40 Member States
  • Rules of Procedure: Standard UN4MUN
  • Chairs: TBA
